About

Food Donation is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste and alleviating hunger in communities. Our mission is to connect donors with surplus food to those in need.

How It Works

  1. Donors sign up and post details of available food items.
  2. Acceptors browse the list of available food items and request those they need.
  3. Donors and acceptors coordinate the pickup or delivery of food items.
